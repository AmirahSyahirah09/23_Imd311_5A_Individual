TEXAS CHICKEN



I started working as a cashier after I finished SPM. At first I didn't want to work as a cashier because I was shy to face customers. If you work in a factory,
you don't have to deal with people because you focus on the same job. But I braved myself to work as a cashier at
Texas Chicke in Klang Parade, Selangor.

Before being hired as a cashier, I was asked to answer some math questions. After that I was given a special uniform for cashier workers only.
The next day I was asked to come early at 9 am. Because I was still new, I was given training before opening my own counter.
If you have opened your own counter, if there is a short, you will cut your salary. This is where I learned many different types of customers.
I used to get angry because the food arrived late. It is also possible for a cashier to get angry about something that has nothing to do with it.
I once got angry because another staff gave the wrong food to a customer. I'm always hit huhu.
I've also been short up to RM30 as long as I've been working as a cashier. Thank goodness it didn't reach a hundred.
That's my experience working as a cashier. There are joys and sorrows that I went through working as a cashier..
